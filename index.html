Willing in a life not enlightened yet, is a psychological impulse that can cause a person to jump to a conclusion , before having sufficient information to draw the logically appropriate conclusion.

This impulse makes our judgment less accurate than it otherwise would be.It runs silently in the background of life. It is a psychological obstacle to critical thinking or as I say to “endless happiness that contains permanent sadness”. You see above the subset of knowledge claims that some of us haven’t had.

What is this impulse ? Well this is a question that has been asked by some people in any given time.

Only asking this question can counteract the effect of the impulse. Most of us think that life is nothing without this impulse. Well not necessarily nothing but could be less significant. What does being significant mean ? Does it really lead the way ? you should know what I meant by the way.

Most of us have been seeking for something ‘significant’. Because we have all been thought or manipulated that way. I have no idea who or what manipulated us . or for what ? The only thing I can assume that there is something going on and it was designed to cause us not to examine our truth , our values , our beliefs or rather what point of the life we are at. Its sounds might make no sense .

When you don’t make a conscious effort to examine all the sides of an issue , you don’t come up with sufficient assumptions. You are not ready to decide. So you should stay back . Contrary to what you should do , you go ahead. You didn’t reveal anything regarding the issue yet. But you think the conclusion you just jump to is infallible . but you don’t know that there is a block that lurks within it. But the way you choose is much easier and faster than the way requiring an effort. And this is what we have been desired to do by the monster that I just called as the impulse. Or the design that was designed by a thing. If you come across this monster deriving from our currently truths which are supplied by our wrong knowledges , never let it go. Try to live with it . Make a good friend. Sometime go and get some drink together. But never let it go. Just tell it that you had to work together to stand against ‘designers’. I can assure you once you make a good friend with it, you are so going to taste the real freedom.

Tugce BAYDAR
